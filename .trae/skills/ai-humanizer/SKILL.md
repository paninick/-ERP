# AI Text Humanizer

Improves **AI-generated text** to sound more human, more natural, less robotic. Adds proper sentence variation, conversational tone, contractions, and natural phrasing that makes text undetectable by AI detectors.

## What It Does

- Breaks up long robotic sentences into shorter natural chunks
- Adds sentence length variation (some short, some longer) instead of uniform length
- Introduces natural contractions (don't, can't, wouldn't) instead of full forms everywhere
- Uses conversational phrasing that sounds like a real person wrote it
- Maintains original meaning completely - doesn't change what you said, just how you said it
- Reduces detectability by AI content detectors

## When to Use

- You have AI-generated text that needs to sound more human
- You need to pass AI detection for academic/work content
- You want your writing to have more natural variation
- You need to make the tone more conversational instead of formal robotic

## Humanization Principles

1. **Sentence Length Variation**: Mix short 1- clause sentences with longer ones, don't make all sentences the same length
2. **Contractions**: Use common contractions naturally (don't instead of do not) where appropriate
3. **Vocabulary Variation**: Replace overly formal words with more conversational alternatives where appropriate
4. **Fragments**: Allow occasional sentence fragments like real writing instead of always requiring complete sentences
5. **Transitions**: Natural transitions between paragraphs like real writing, not forced academic transitions
6. **Meaning Preservation**: Always keep the original meaning 100% - don't change the content, just the style

## Example

**Original AI text:**
> Artificial intelligence has been increasingly utilized in order to assist human beings with content creation. This technological advancement enables individuals to create content more efficiently than was previously possible.

**After humanization:**
> AI has become more and more used to help people create content. It helps us make content faster than we could before.

## Usage

1. User provides the AI-generated text
2. Apply the humanization principles above to rewrite
3. Output the humanized version that's ready to use

## Output Requirements

- Keep all original information intact
- Only change style, not content
- Maintain the same structure and paragraph breaks
- Deliver natural sounding text that passes AI detection
